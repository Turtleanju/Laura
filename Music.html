My Music!


I love music!! I spend about half my week listening to music! I listen to all sorts of music from Death Industrial to Primus. My mental state plays a great role in my music taste and so when I decided I was going to make a small music project to express it I created Festering Cereberal Wound. I don't think it's the best music I could have created but it expresses a lot of my anxiety that I had around the political climate of the USA and my growing fear that I would be unsafe in the world as a trans woman and around a country with regular mass shootings. you can listen to it on Bandcamp! My music will always be free because art is for everyone!!
